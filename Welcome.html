What We Do For You

For all patrons, volunteers, and staff:  We borrow library materials and obtain photocopies of articles from other libraries. This service is available for any items not owned by the Prince William Public Libraries (PWPL).

For other library systems: We lend books and provide photocopies of articles from our collections.

How You Receive Items

Hold for Pickup: All books can be held for you at one of our eleven library locations.

In Library Use: All in library use materials can be held for you at one of our eleven locations. Exception: All microforms can be held at Bull Run Regional Library or Chinn Park Regional Library, where microform readers are available.

Mail to Address: Photocopies and other non-returnable items can be mailed to your address.

For more information about ILL (eligibility, waiting period, etc.), go to our ILL Policy.

Welcome to ILLiad
ILLiad is our web based InterLibrary Loan service. You can place requests for books, microforms and photocopies of articles, check the status of your requests, and communicate with the InterLibrary Loan office.
To be eligible to use ILLiad you must:
  • 1) live, work or own property in Prince William County or the City of Manassas
  • 2) have less than $20.00 in library fines or fees on your library record
